FAQ

Questions we
hear often

Still unsure? Drop us a WhatsApp and we'll answer straight away.

Ask Us Anything
Do I need to provide content and photos? +
Not necessarily. We can write the text for your site as part of the Professional and Business plans. For photos, we use high-quality stock images that match your business — or you can send us your own if you have them.
How long will my website take? +
Most landing pages are done in 7–10 days. Full business websites take 10–14 days. We'll give you a clear timeline before we start and stick to it.
Will I be able to update the site myself? +
Yes. We build with a simple content management system and give you a short training session so you can update text, photos, and prices yourself — without needing to call us for every small change.
What if I'm not happy with the design? +
Before we build anything, we show you a design mockup. If you don't love it, we revise it — we include two full rounds of revisions in every plan. Your satisfaction is the whole point.
Do you work with businesses outside Nairobi? +
Absolutely. We work with businesses across Greater Houston — Sugar Land, Katy, The Woodlands, Pearland, Pasadena, and beyond. Everything is handled remotely by phone, email, and video call, so location is no barrier at all.
What happens after the website is launched? +
We don't just disappear. Every plan includes a post-launch support period. We also offer a monthly maintenance plan ($79/month) if you'd like someone to keep the site updated and running smoothly long-term.
